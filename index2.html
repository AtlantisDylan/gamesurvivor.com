About The Game

Monster Survivors is an intense, action-packed survival game where you are pitted against relentless hordes of monstrous creatures. Your only goal is to survive for as long as you can. As you defeat enemies, you'll gain experience, level up, and unlock powerful new abilities and weapons. Choose your upgrades wisely to build the ultimate monster-slaying machine and climb the leaderboards!

How to Play

The controls are simple, but the challenge is immense. Use your mouse or keyboard to navigate your character and automatically attack the nearest enemies. Focus on dodging enemy attacks while collecting gems to level up. Each level up presents you with a choice of new skills or upgrades to enhance your chances of survival.

Key Features

  • Endless waves of diverse monsters to battle.
  • A deep progression system with unique skills and upgrades.
  • Fast-paced, responsive gameplay optimized for all devices.
  • Compete for the top spot on the global leaderboards.
  • Free to play, anytime, anywhere.
